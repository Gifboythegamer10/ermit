<h3>
  Introduction to Ermit
</h3>

Hi, I'm Gifboy! I've started Ermit with a couple of my friends- it's basically a huge video, music, and game sharing platform between us.
It's going to have a proper UI, designed by me, and a proper sharing hub, made by my friends and me.

We want to be able to release Ermit to the world someday, but software development (and buying Raspberry Pis for an Ermit TV maybe??) won't come freely, and so that's where
you come in!

You can help this small project by doing what you can do make this
thing better for all of us!
Contribute in the wiki! Highlight some issues! Join the discord! Et cetera!!
<h3>
  So, why Ermit?
</h3>

Ermit's going to hopefully have a massive community of makers, testers, consumers, and programmers- the all-around creative! It'll be fun to see what you'll do with this
creation power! Maybe you'll build a game, share it to Ermit, and people around the world with access to the Ermit TV (it could exist with your support!) or just the app/website
(we don't know yet) will be able to get it and play it!

You'll be able to get constructive feedback, and if you're lucky, win Ermit Creator of the Year (maybe if we get enough support!! wink wink nudge nudge) and get a trophy!
Who knows? It'll be awesome!!
